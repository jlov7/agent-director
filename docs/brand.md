## Voice
- Cinematic, technical, and confident.
- Short verbs. Clear outcomes.
- Never hand-wavy about time or replay semantics.

## Visual tone
- Night mode by default.
- Accent colors map to step types.
- Timeline and graph feel like instruments, not dashboards.
