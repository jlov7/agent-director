# Demo Script (90 seconds)

## Scene 1 — “Show me the run” (0:00–0:15)
**Narration:** “This is Agent Director. It’s a cinematic, chat-native debugger for AI agents.”
- Optional: use the **Director briefing** to start the Guided Tour or Story mode, then pause to take over.
- Load the latest trace.
- Hit Play; point out wall-clock accuracy and overlap lanes.

## Scene 2 — “Spot the bottleneck” (0:15–0:30)
**Narration:** “The Insight Strip surfaces the story in seconds.”
- Click the top latency chip.
- Inspector opens with redaction-by-default.

## Scene 3 — “Reveal only what’s safe” (0:30–0:40)
**Narration:** “Sensitive data stays redacted unless you explicitly reveal.”
- Toggle Safe Export (on).
- Highlight that raw mode is disabled.

## Scene 4 — “Morph to Flow” (0:40–0:55)
**Narration:** “Now the timeline morphs into the graph — same run, new lens.”
- Toggle Flow.
- Enable I/O edges. Explain semantic layers.

## Scene 5 — “Director’s Cut” (0:55–1:10)
**Narration:** “Replay from a step and compare the branch.”
- Replay from a tool step (Hybrid default).
- Switch to Compare.
- Show ghost overlay + diff summary.

## Scene 6 — “Export the diff” (1:10–1:20)
**Narration:** “Export the diff summary for audits and team review.”
- Click Export diff.

## Scene 7 — “Close” (1:20–1:30)
**Narration:** “Watch your agent think. Then direct it.”
